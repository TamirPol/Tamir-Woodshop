Joineries

Steps

There are multiple steps that I took to create the Joineries, which include both planning and execution steps.

  1. Research
    2. Just like with the barbeque scraper, the first step is research. In this project what I researched was different types of joints and I familiarized myself with them. I have learned about the Miter Joint, Half Lap Joint, Dovetail Joint, Butt Joint, Pocket Screw, Mortise and Tenon, Tongue and Groove, Dowel Joint, Dado, Biscuit, and Rabbet. I have seached up their uses, their names, how they look, and their definitions. To learn about them as well, go to the research page.
  2. Sketchup

    3. Now it is time to do sketchup. SketchUp is a 3D modeling computer program for a wide range of drawing applications such as architectural, interior design, and landscape architecture, but can also be used for many more applications. This is how my design looks on SketchUp.(SketchUp site)
