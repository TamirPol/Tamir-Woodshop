Final Design

After a few days of working on my barbeque scraper it is now complete. I have learned a lot from this project such as how to use tools and how to work with wood. This was an extremely fun project and I am waiting to continue creating more wood project in tech class in febuary. One thing I believe I could have done a bit better was the planning of my scraper. I believe I could've given more thoughts to my handle for things such as size of hole and the functionality of the handle. Something I believe I did well was hand sanding. My last step was sanding the entire project and I did this for multiple hours. I have used different kinds of sand paper (120, 150, 180, 220), on every edge for a long time until there were no scratches left and all the sides were very smooth.
