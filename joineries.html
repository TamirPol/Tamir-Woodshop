Joineries

My Second Woodworking Project

After finishing my first project, the barbeque scraper, it is time to move onto something harder. Joineries. In this project I am going to cut multiple different joints which include: the butt joint, half lap joint, miter joint, 2 dovetail joints, and the mortise and tenon joint into one woodpiece as shown in the sketchup image.
