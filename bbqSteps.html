BBQ Scraper

Steps

There are multiple steps that I took to create the barbeque scraper, which include both planning and execution steps.

  1. The first step you have to take in creating the scraper is drawing a 3d version of the barbeque scraper on sketchup. SketchUp is a 3D modeling computer program for a wide range of drawing applications such as architectural, interior design, and landscape architecture, but can also be used for many more applications. This is how my design looks.

  2. The second step you have to take is drawing a real-life size diagram of your barbeque scraper on a long piece of paper.
